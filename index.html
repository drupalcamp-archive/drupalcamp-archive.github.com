A Drupalcamp is a one to two day event that focuses on many aspects of Drupal in one location. Its focus is knowledge sharing amongst the community. Many of the camps have their own websites which can be a valuable source of information in themselves.

Most Drupalcamps will have an assicated website that can contain useful information that can be lost when the site is replaced or removed.

This site is an archive of Drupalcamp sites, which have been made static. if you have a Drupalcamp website, you can add it to this archive easily.
